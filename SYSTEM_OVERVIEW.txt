FOREX STRATEGY:
- D1 + H4 must agree on trend direction (hard gate)
- H1 looks for pullback entries WITH the trend only
- Countertrend trades blocked entirely
- RSI, MACD, SMA20/50, volume confirmation on H1
- 1.5x ATR stop loss, 2x ATR take profit (1:1.33 R:R)
- 2% risk per trade, max 5 open positions
- Confidence threshold 0.35 minimum
- Skips scanning when forex market is closed

OPTIONS STRATEGY:
- Same HTF trend filter on underlying stock (D1+H4 gate)
- Calls in uptrend, puts in downtrend
- Contract selection: 7-14 DTE, near ATM, min 100 open interest, max 15% spread
- Max $500 premium per trade
- Exit at 50% profit or 40% loss on premium
- Force close 2 days before expiry
- Max 3 open option positions
- No trading first/last 15 min of market
- Limit orders only (not market orders)
